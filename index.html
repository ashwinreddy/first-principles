1 Introduction

Welcome to this free, open-source, online book First Principles! If you’re like me, you’ve often tried to learn more about interesting topics in various fields only to find yourself looking at lots of jargon. This book will hopefully contain explanations that do not obscure interesting ideas more than neccessary. I also hope that in making this book public that:

  • I will be able to test whether I truly understand certain concepts by explaining them clearly, carefully, and concisely.
  • I will force myself to a higher standard of quality, knowing that anyone can read it
  • others will benefit from having such explanations
